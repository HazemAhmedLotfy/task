Announcement of Competition

About The Competition

This competition is an attempt to encourage young designers to set benchmark for design in Egypt. Building on the outstanding success of the previous D+I Competitions, EFEC & CWWFI proudly announce the launch of the 5th round under the direction of the international renowned Italian designer Giulio Cappellini who will be a main Jury member . All designers below the age of 35 are invited to participate with their product design ideas and submit their best work. The designs presented must address the functional needs for people for a better life. The project has to include furnishings for indoor & outdoor furniture such as - but not limited to - chairs, tables, sofas, armchairs, lamps, hangers, office furniture … etc

How to Participate
To participate, designers should follow the following steps:
  • Create your account on the competition online platform
  • Finalize payment of registration fees of L.E 250 At EFEC premises 90B Road 105, Maadi - Cairo – Egypt. Or through our bank account for more info please contact Tel: +202 2528 50 36 (Ext:106)
  • Upload your project drawings on your account (in high-resolution PDF in horizontal A3 format, maximum 3 pages and maximum 8MB, containing images, draft and a short description explaining concept, product name, function, materials and technology )
  • After first phase selection, the winners have to confirm within 72 hours, to participate in Furnex & The Home exhibition and guarantee availability of the prototype on the deadlines shown in the timetable
  • Winners of phase one are required to pay a LE 750 (Seven hundred and fifty Egyptian Pounds) insurance fee to confirm commitment to the next phase of the competition. If they fail to pay the money in time they risk dismissal from the next phase.
  • Participants shall submit proof of completion (can be photos or videos) of their prototypes on or before the specified dates to ensure continued reservation of their space in the local exhibition. Failure to do so may risk cancellation of their reservation.
  • Participants must be available to deliver their products on the construction day of Furnex & The Home exhibition. Also participants are responsible for stand dismantling on the last day of the exhibition.
  • Participants must ensure that their booth is manned with someone capable of providing the relevant information on what is exhibited during all days of the Fair. (Otherwise their booth will be subject to closure by the organizers).
What Participants Get during the local exhibition
The organizers are responsible for providing the following:
  • Each participant will be allocated an exhibition booth equipped with an electrical connection and a lighting fixture. All else will be the responsibility of the exhibitor.
  • Each booth will carry the name of the designer as indicated in registration forms
  • A collective brochure introducing all designers with a short introduction on their projects will be published and distributed during the event. (Any designer who cannot meet deadline of receiving needed information will not be included)
Terms & Conditions
To participate, designers should follow the following steps:
  • All the presented product ideas have to be the original work of the applicant and not copied nor borrowed, otherwise they will be subject to exclusion
  • Upon selection for participation in Phase Two, ONLY finished prototypes will be eligible for participation in the exhibition. Failure to complete the prototype in the specified time will result in cancellation of the designer’s participation and thus they lose their insurance fee and booth.
  • Registration fees & insurance fees are not refunded in case designer cancelled his/her participation in a later stage of the project.
  • By filling the competition application the participants give the legal rights to the organizers to use the imagery, information, and process material for publishing, documentations and promotional purposes.
